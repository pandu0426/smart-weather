About SmartWeather

A clean, fast weather tool built to demonstrate full-stack development with Django.

What it does

SmartWeather is a real-time weather dashboard that lets you look up current conditions for any city in the world. Search by city name to instantly see temperature, humidity, wind speed, weather description, and a matching sky icon.

The interface is designed around clarity — no clutter, no unnecessary data, just the conditions you actually need to know.
