How it works

SmartWeather queries the OpenWeatherMap API server-side and renders your results instantly — no JavaScript, no loading states.

1
Type a city name into the search bar — any city, anywhere in the world.
2
Hit Search and Django fetches live data from the weather API in real time.
3
See your results — temperature, humidity, wind speed, sky condition, and day.
